Good Coffee, Great Coffee

Wake up and smell the coffee because TapBlaze's newest simulation cooking game, Good Coffee, Great Coffee, is finally here. Prepare delicious drinks, experiment with new flavors, decorate your shop, and manage cafe costs to become the best barista in town!

Fulfill quirky customer orders with coffee drinks that look and taste delicious! Upgrade your shop with new decorations and equipment to increase your cafe profit. Make friends, rivals, and more when locals visit your shop. Listen to their stories and inspire the town with your coffee-making skills!

Story

Trials

The main storyline follows a set of challenges that will test your coffee-making abilites. The World Coffee Association will test you on your knowledge of each basic skill you earn as you level up, by sending judges to run the Trials. Every trial will showcase a different ability, so be prepared! The Main story progresses through both the Trials and special quests.
