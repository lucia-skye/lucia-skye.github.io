Wake up and smell the coffee because TapBlaze's newest simulation cooking game, Good Coffee, Great Coffee, is finally here. Prepare delicious drinks, experiment with new flavors, decorate your shop, and manage cafe costs to become the best barista in town!

Fulfill quirky customer orders with coffee drinks that look and taste delicious! Upgrade your shop with new decorations and equipment to increase your cafe profit. Make friends, rivals, and more when locals visit your shop. Listen to their stories and inspire the town with your coffee-making skills!

Story

Trials

The main storyline follows a set of challenges that will test your coffee-making abilites. The World Coffee Association will test you on your knowledge of each basic skill you earn as you level up, by sending judges to run the Trials. Every trial will showcase a different ability, so be prepared! The main story progresses through both the Trials and special quests.Your Trials will be:

  1. Latte Art - There'll be three different styles of latte art for you to master in order for you to pass this trial!
  2. Flavoured Drinks - There are three flavours to add to your repository, and you'll learn to work with all three to get to the next and final trial.
  3. Final Test - The last challenge will test your ability to accurately fulfill customer orders and mix up some complex drinks!

Side Stories

In addition to the main storyline, there is another set of quests and side characters that are introduced on various in-game days. When you interact with them, you can learn more about both them and some of the events happening around town!

Characters

There are a few key characters introduced throughout the story, as you level up your cafe.
